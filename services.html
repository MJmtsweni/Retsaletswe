Services

We cater for the following services

Productions

A season run: Retsaletswe House Production, GaboDArts together with Pharatlhatle Theatre gives community groups a platform to stage their work and sell tickets with the intension for the local artists to see business in what they do, develop independency and self-reliance as most of them wait for competitions and government hand-outs, not realising that their a business, however, proposals are not required but the fieldwork must see the work first and he or she decides if the work is decent to be seen by audience and for Retsaletswe, GaboDArts to be a partner, remember we want to give the audience experience, excellency and quality work. The production may be Drama, Dance and music or any other form of performing Arts.


  • The production will be given 4 days season run.
  • Pharatlhatle theatre will take 40% from the tickets sales.
  • Will provide artist aid if necessary.
  • Will provide with theatre(equipment and a space for performance).
  • Pharatlhatle and the artist will go 50/50 on advertisement of the product.

Workshops

Since there is Pharatlhatlhe Theatre in Mamusa, we see the need to give Art Education in form of workshops maintain quality work. We offer workshops in:


  • Acting
  • Directing
  • Writing
  • Art in business
  • Theatre-making

Set-Work

We recognise that not all learners can learn best only from reading or listening the teacher in class but to carter learners that do need visual, to see in order to understand


We offer school plays “set-work”, from grade 10- 12.


The arrangement can be done between the relevant teacher and Retsaletswe house Production.

  • Smoke machine

Note: We provide our technical Person to handle all the equipment and only He or She should do that. The pricing and number of equipment should be known when you hire.
